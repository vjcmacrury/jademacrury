about me

My name is Jade MacRury and I am a unifier, able to see the different strands in a situation, and reason out a way to draw them together.

I believe every experience, no matter how mundane, brings something of value and that life needs to be experienced in order to be fully lived.

To this end, I have collected experiences. Thirteen years in customer service. Two years in official and eleven in unofficial but equally relevant IT support roles.

I've been a leader, a teacher, a coach, a writer, an editor, a photojournalist, a student, a mentee, a researcher.

I'm an extensive traveller, a dreamer and a dreamcatcher - having gone to nearly every single country I've dreamed of visiting as a child and achieved nearly every single dream.

My experiences provide a rich tapestry of inspiration for all of my creations. If you are like me or you love what I create, hit me a message and let me code your ideas to life.
