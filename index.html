about me

My name is Jade MacRury and I am a unifier, able to see the different strands in a situation, and reason out a way to draw them together. I believe every experience, no matter how mundane, brings something of value and that life needs to be experienced in order to be lived.

To this end, I have collected experiences. Thirteen years in customer service. Two years in official and eleven in unofficial but equally relevant IT support roles. I've been a leader, a teacher, a coach, a writer, an editor, a photojournalist, a student, a mentee, a researcher.
